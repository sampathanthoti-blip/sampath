Anime Sketchbook

Brief content visible, double tap to read full content.
Full content visible, double tap to read brief content.

Claire Hudson was born in the stunning coastal city of Vancouver, British Columbia and went to university in Montreal, where she studied art and design. From there she returned to Vancouver, through a circuitous route that took her to Chicago, San Francisco and then Seattle. She now lives in downtown Vancouver with her husband, two children and a stray cat.

Claires creative touch has always been something she had. Even as a young child she loved to draw, sketch and colour, but it was a chance meeting with an old classmate that introduced her to adult coloring books and their potential to help with concentration, attention to detail and relaxation.

Now she has developed a whole range of these books that are aimed at helping stressed out people to relax and enjoy life, even if its only for a few minutes a day. In addition to these, Claire has also designed notebooks that are perfect for scribbling thoughts, ideas and other notions that come to mind.

She hopes that these books, combined, will be a way for others to change the way they view the world in general and spend their down time. They have been made specifically so that people can create something they can be proud of and reinvigorate themselves in the process.

In her spare time, Claire loves cooking, going to the movies and spending time with her children and husband. They regularly head out onto the water or into the hills around their home, enjoying the fresh air and wildlife that is in abundance where they live.
